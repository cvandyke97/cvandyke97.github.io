Superfund Sites in the U.S.

This map depicts the locations of sites in the Lower 48 appearing on the Environmental Protection Agency's National Priorities List (NPL). The NPL contains sites at which there are known or threatened releases of hazardous substances, pollutants, or other contaminants that are capable of endangering human health and the environment.

QGIS was used to perform hexbin mapping.

  • See the map: Superfund Sites
  • Data Source: NASA Socioeconomic Data and Applications Center (SEDAC)

Carbon Dioxide Emissions from U.S. Power Plants

This map displays carbon dioxide emissions from U.S. power plants. When zoomed out, the map depicts CO2 emissions per square kilometer at the county level. Users can click on individual counties to view detailed emissions data. At larger scales, users can determine the level of emissions from individual facilities.

Data were initally mapped in QGIS before they were exported to Carto for additional processing

  • See the map: Carbon Dioxide Emissions from U.S. Power Plants
  • Data Source: Environmental Protection Agency
